The Coding Robot

Roblotics is an educational toy designed to give young children a cheaper and more tactile introduction to coding logic and robotics. Through small, literal coding blocks, children can put together strings of commands to instruct a small robot to perform basic tasks like move forward, spin, or even dance.

About Roblotics

Our Mission

Roblotics is designed with one focus in mind: creating educational, fun, and inexpensive toys to help children of all backgrounds gain exposure to and spark interest in computer science and computational thinking.


Background

There is a constant, widening gap regarding socioeconomic backgrounds in the major STEM fields, and while companies and individuals have been striving to address this, many individuals attribute their disinterest due to a lack of exposure from a young age. We are a team of women currently enrolled in college STEM programs, and if you were to ask any of us what drove us to our respective studies, we would all respond with stories from our childhoods in which science and technology played a large role in our self interests and experiences, and how we wished to use those experiences to make a positive impact in the world. Our goal is to therefore bring those life changing moments to the next generation without the barriers of cost or commitment that normally comes with such a budding field.
