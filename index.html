The Coding Robot

Roblotics is an educational toy designed to give young children a cheaper, and more tactile introduction to coding logic and robotics. Through small, literal coding blocks, children can put together strings of commands to instruct a small robot to perform basic tasks like move forward, spin, or even dance.
